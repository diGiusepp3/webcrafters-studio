Self-Hosting AI Generated Applications

Self-hosting means you run your application on your own infrastructure. With Webcrafters Studio, every generated app can be deployed independently.

Why self-hosting matters

  • No vendor lock-in
  • Full control over infrastructure
  • Compliance and data ownership
  • Scales beyond demos

How Webcrafters Studio enables self-hosting

Studio generates real frontend and backend projects. You receive all source files, configuration and structure needed for deployment.

Where can you deploy?

  • Your own VPS or dedicated server
  • On-premise infrastructure
  • Cloud providers of your choice
  • Container-based environments
